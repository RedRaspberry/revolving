# Simple Machines

# Getting Started
Like all create mod playthroughs, you should start by getting some Andesite Alloy. This can be crafted more efficiently in a mixer later, so don't make too much by hand.

You'll want the typical starter Create setup of at least a Mechanical Press and a Mixer with some source of SU, probably waterwheel spam.

Remember, normal **Create** contraptions do not give free energy in this modpack! Drills, Saws, and other components will not function on non-physically simulated contraptions, since they cannot receive rotational power. If you want to have a drill on a contraption, you will need to use the equivalent physics bearing and provide power to the drill on the sublevel.

Your short term goals should be:
- Entering the Nether
- Getting lots of Redstone and Quartz to make Rose Quartz and Electron Tubes
- Capturing Blazes to make Blaze Burners
- Stockpiling Copper and Zinc to make Brass
- Building a 5x5 Mechanical Crafter
- Getting a pair of Crushing Wheels
