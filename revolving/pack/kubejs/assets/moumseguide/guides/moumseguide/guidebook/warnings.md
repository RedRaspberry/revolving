# Known Bugs and Issues

# Simulated Contraptions
You should never form a simulated contraption out of existing Immersive Engineering or Immersive Petroleum multiblock structures.

Likewise, you should never return a simulated contraption that contains IE/IP multiblock structures to worldspace.

You will probably end up duplicating a bunch of blocks if you attempt this, or you might crash the game. I trust you to not abuse this knowledge. I have done my best to prevent situations where you could even do this in the first place, but it's still possible.

You are allowed to form IE multiblocks while they are already made of simulated blocks.
