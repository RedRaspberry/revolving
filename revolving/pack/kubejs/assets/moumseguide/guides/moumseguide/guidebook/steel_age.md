# Getting Started
Now that you have a massive amount of SU from Steam Engines, you can truly begin the process of industrializing the world. The first step towards this will be to create Coal Coke, a more efficient form of Coal which burns hotter. The process of creating Coal Coke also releases volatile compounds in the form of Carbon Dioxide and Creosote, which you can either dump or store for later. 
After amassing some Coal Coke, you can crush it into dust, and use it in a Blast Furnace, along with Iron and Limestone Dust
