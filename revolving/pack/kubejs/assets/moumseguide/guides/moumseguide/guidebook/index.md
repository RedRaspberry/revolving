# Info

Hello <PlayerName />! 

This modpack is centered around **Create** and **Immersive Engineering**, with a focus on **realistic electrical networks**, **trains, cars, other transportation methods**, and **chemical processing**. However, we remain mostly kitchen-sink rather than "expert pack". As such, there are no quests, you can play at your own pace, this guidebook only exists to tell you what machines are available to mess around with, how to use them, and to give a rough sense of progression milestones.
<br/>

# Getting Started

The major progression points in this pack are:

1. Basic Create mod rotary power setup, using waterwheels or windmills
2. Upgrading to steam power, creating some basic assembly lines
3. Setting up basic electricity with steam turbines
4. Finding ore deposits around the world to obtain a steady supply of basic resources, and shipping them back home with trains
5. Finding an oil deposit, and extracting it with a derrick and pumpjack, before processing it into various petroleum distillates with a distillation tower
6. Creating your first plastics
7. Using electric power to process aluminum, and beginning large scale chemical processing
8. Creating advanced control circuitry for your factory and electric grid
9. Upgrading to nuclear power and possibly visiting the stars, if any good create-aeronautics space addons come out between now and the time somebody gets here
