# Plastic
# Getting Started
Plastic is a versitile material produced from oil byproducts. It is used in the production of various electronic components, can be used as an alternative material for piping, finds use as a durable material capable of standing up to gale-force winds in anemometers. It can also be used to craft certain decorative blocks. Automating a steady supply of plastic should be a priority. Your first plastics will be made from propylene (polypropylene).

The **Chemica** mod adds a varity of other plastics, most of which have been made interchangeable with polypropylene in recipes. 

Instead of adding a million materials with only a single use, these other plastics now have specific characteristics that make them more suited for certain tasks, which is represented by those materials giving increased yield for specific crafting recipes. These are meant to be automation challenges that perhaps one player on a server will specialize into.

- PVC increases yield when making plastic pipes and certain electric components.
- Carbon Fiber is used as plating for endgame armor ??
- Nylon can be used to make string and tough fabric.
