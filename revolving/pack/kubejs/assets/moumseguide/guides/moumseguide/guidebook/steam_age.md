# Steam Machines

# Getting Started
<Row>
<RecipeFor id="create:steam_engine"/>
For now, you should begin by creating a set of <ItemLink id="create:steam_engine" />s using your supply of Brass, <br/>
and heat them with <ItemLink id="create:blaze_burner" />s fueled with Coal or Charcoal. 
Later, you will be able to use better fuels, like <ItemLink id="tfmg:coal_coke" /> and the mythical <ItemLink id="create:blaze_cake" />. <br/>
</Row>

You should already have a steady supply of Coal by means of an ore deposit and train, but if not, you can create a simple tree farm for Charcoal. Eventually, you will need Coal and not Charcoal in order to create Coal Coke, so keep that in mind.

These steam engines will make far more SU than your early waterwheel and windmill setups ever could, and are a key step towards future industrial breakthroughs.
